About this place

The holiday cottage is located 9 km from the town of Idrija, in an idyllic countryside in a forest-quiet paradise, on the edge of the landscape park of Zgornja Idrijca, independently next to our home house. The location is the starting point for cycling, hiking, fishing in the Idrijca and Soča rivers, hunting, mushroom picking and more. Next to the house (25 m away) there is the signposted Slovenska Hiking Trail Via Alpina. Access to the cottage is arranged. You park your vehicle under the large covered parking within the holiday cottage for free.
